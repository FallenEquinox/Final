The story of Lurelin Village

Lurelin Village is a seaside town located at the Western end of Faron Highroad in East Necluda. Lrelin is a coastal fishing settlement. The village is led and made possible by Rozel, a retired fishermen and elder who shares his wisdoms of areas of interest. During the upheval, villagers were forced to scatter across hyrule to avoid the pirates that reckonded their onces peaceful living. When all hope was lost, a hero swooped in and saved the village not only giving the people hope for their futures on the coast, but the courage to open their doors to the public to Experience the life by the sea! Thanks to the dedication of the knight, the village overflows with playful aura and laughter!
