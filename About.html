About Lurelin Village

Lurelin Village has had many challenges, starting with 100 years ago. While we were spared to the Calamity of Ganon, we didn't know what was in store when the Upheveal happened.

Pirates took over our village, forcing us to flee home and scatter across Hyrule. Until a hero with a legendary sword saved us and rebuilt our village from the ground up.
